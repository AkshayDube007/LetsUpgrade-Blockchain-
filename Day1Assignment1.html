2. What is the core problem, Blockchain trying to solve?

Normally in any type of transaction has middle men/agents in the transaction and due to this there is a risk of information getting transmitted to other parties. Blockchain solves this problem as it's an encrypted, secure, decentralized form of network and this is the core problem that Blockchain is solving today.

The core problems that Blockchain solves

1. No Hacking

2. Huge Security boost

3. Verifiable

4. Data Transparency

5. Decentralisation of authority to keey the data safe

3. Whar are the few features that Blockchain will give you?

Blockchain is a mix of technology having "DISTRIBUTED DATABASE" & "CRYPTOGRAPHY" used together to create much wanted transparency and trust in the system by eradicating middle man.

It delivers below mentioned features:

Verifiable - It basically inspects who in the community is figuring unwanted issues to access the data and makes that block to retrieve the data from the other blocks chained with it.

Unchangeable - The data secured with blockchain concept is unchangeable as it is been strored/accessed by numerous systems.

Tamper-proof - The SHA- Hash key for each block wherein you data is stored makes it tamper-proof.

Immutable - Its immposible of alter/modify the data chained in a block of Blockchain.
